Sweet Bro and Hella Bot - sbahj_bot

Sweet Bro and Hella Bot is a twitter bot that cites Sweet Bro and Hella Jeff every hour.

Quick facts:

  • Sweet Bro and Hella Bot runs on SSBot 0.5. Credits to Zach Whalen for the spreadsheet.
  • It cites SBAHJ every hour. This delay will not be adjusted. More frequent comes across as spammy, and too few will come across as uninteresting.
  • It does not currently post any images or respond to others.

I will not make any of the following for the forseeable future:

  • homestuck_bot: Way too much text for me to work around. Also, keep in mind that tweets can only be 140 characters, and some of Homestuck's dialog gets really wordy (or well, all of it). Unless someone can provide me with a dump, this is called off for the forseeable future.
  • problem_sleuth_bot: See homestuck_bot.
